Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our website creation services at Pixel IT Solution.

Your Questions, Answered

Updated: May 6, 2025

What types of websites can you create?

We create a variety of websites, including personal portfolios, business websites, e-commerce platforms, blogs, and custom web applications. Whether you need a simple landing page or a complex online store, we tailor our services to meet your needs.

How much does a website cost?

Our pricing starts at $99 for basic websites and varies based on complexity, features, and customization. For an accurate quote, contact us to discuss your project requirements, and we’ll provide a detailed proposal.

How long does it take to build a website?

Timelines depend on the project scope. A simple website may take 2-4 weeks, while complex sites like e-commerce platforms can take 6-12 weeks. We outline specific timelines in your project agreement and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you provide website hosting and domain services?

We can assist with setting up hosting and domain registration through trusted third-party providers. While we don’t host websites directly, we ensure your site is compatible with your chosen hosting service and guide you through the setup process.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all websites we create are responsive and optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktops. We prioritize user experience across all screen sizes to ensure your site looks great and functions seamlessly.

Can you optimize my website for search engines (SEO)?

Absolutely. We incorporate basic SEO best practices, such as optimized meta tags, fast loading times, and mobile responsiveness. For advanced SEO strategies, we offer additional services like keyword research and content optimization.

What happens if I need changes after the website is completed?

We include a set number of revisions in the project agreement. Post-launch, we offer maintenance packages for updates, bug fixes, or additional features. Contact us to discuss your needs, and we’ll provide a tailored solution.

Do I need to provide content for the website?

You’ll need to provide content like text, images, and branding materials. If you don’t have content ready, we can assist with content creation or recommend professional copywriters and photographers to help bring your vision to life.

How do I get started with my website project?

Contact us via our contact form, email (support@pixelitsolution.com), or phone (+1-800-555-1234). We’ll discuss your goals, provide a free quote, and outline the next steps to kick off your project.

What if I have a question not listed here?

We’re here to help! Reach out to us via email at support@pixelitsolution.com, call us at +1-800-555-1234, or use our contact form. We’ll respond promptly to address your query.
